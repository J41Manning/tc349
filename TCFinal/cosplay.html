Katie Pastor // Photographer for Hire // Grand Rapids, MI

Cosplay

As a cosplayer, myself, I understand the importance of a good photograph to show off a character. During my shoots, the participating cosplayer will part with an assortment of headshots, environmental shots, character shots and details of the costume itself. It's my job to put your character in the best light possible and let the photos speak for all of your hard work.
